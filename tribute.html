Robert Capa, born Endre Friedmann was a Hungarian war photographer and photojournalist.

Early years
Capa was born Endre Friedmann to the Jewish family of Júlia (née Berkovits) and Dezsõ Friedmann in Budapest, Austria-Hungary on October 22, 1913. His mother, Julianna Henrietta Berkovits was a native of Nagykapos (now Ve¾ké Kapušany, Slovakia) and Dezsõ Friedmann came from the Transylvanian village of Csucsa (now Ciucea, Romania). At the age of 18, he was accused of alleged communist sympathies and was forced to flee Hungary.
